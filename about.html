I am Elli

I graduated from the Faculty of Business and tried to build my life in this direction. At the same time can I say that, I am also a bartender. Almost all my education years passed while working in the hospitality sector and became familiar with bartending. I had several project and startup attempts you can see some of them on the Projects page.

During my bachelor studies I worked as a stakeholder analyst, trainer and business consultant. Right after graduation, I moved to Germany and tried to start a master’s degree here, but had to pause it. Because of bureaucratic reasons, more precisely limited work permission, I worked for a few years as a worker in agriculture. In the last year, I returned to bartending to continue my life. In parallel, I completed a one-semester specialization program in Business Intelligence.

My personal view on business

It can be explained with two main factors. The first one is a liberal approach. In business processes we can call this an agile approach. The second factor is the importance of data and detail-based decisions. I believe that intuitive decisions should always be tested with real metrics, different scenarios and only then applied. Because of this second factor, I chose my last specialization in this field. My final research project in that program also touched the topic of agile approach.

Mathematical

This way of thinking about business is often called “mathematical.” Yes, I try always to have a mathematical way of thinking. Also find it interesting to think about mathematics itself. I personally believe that mathematics is discovered, not invented. More precisely, the arguments for discovery seem stronger to me than the others. In business, I cannot say that this approach is always the most successful one. For example.

Once a friend said about the equation “0.(9) = 1”: “You see, even mathematics is not mathematical.” This is not an argument to ignore or reject easily. He said this during a meeting of a project we were working on. I understand that there must be a balance, but I believe that mathematical thinking should have a very large place in this balance.

Mixology

About my bartending experience, had the chance to work as a bartender in three different countries and seven different cities, sometimes for very short periods. This was truly interesting. The mixology part of this job is a hobby for me. Creating new tastes with different flavors is something I strongly recommend to anyone reading this. You can discover your own liqueur recipes and create flavors that perfectly match your taste. It is easier than it sounds.

Rock'n Roll

Rock’n’roll is also one of the things that represents me, so I want to touch this topic as well. In the future, I plan to add a new section to this page. It will include many amazing, not very famous, mostly acoustic recordings. I want to collect them on one page. Personally, I prefer rock’n’roll, alternative rock, and alternative music in general. Because of this, I also plan to share discoveries that were created with small budgets, few instruments, and minimal intervention.
